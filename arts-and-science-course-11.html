B.Sc. Computer Technology

B.Sc. Computer Technology is an undergraduate program that focuses on the study of computer hardware, software, and systems integration. Students learn about computer architecture, digital electronics, and computer networks.

Scope

1. Hardware Engineer 2. Systems Administrator 3. Network Technician 4. Technical Support Specialist 5. Embedded Systems Engineer 6. IT Sales Representative 7. IT Trainer
