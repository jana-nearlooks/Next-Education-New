BA LLB

BA LLB is an integrated undergraduate course that combines the study of Bachelor of Arts (BA) and Bachelor of Laws (LLB). It provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the legal system along with insights into various subjects of arts.

Scope

1. Lawyer, 2. Legal Advisor, 3. Legal Consultant, 4. Judge (after further studies), 5. Corporate Counsel, 6. Human Rights Advocate, 7. Law Professor

Salary

₹3,00,000 - ₹6,00,000 /Per Year


B.Com LLB

B.Com LLB is an integrated undergraduate course that combines the study of Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) and Bachelor of Laws (LLB). It provides students with a strong foundation in both commerce and law, preparing them for a career in legal practice with a focus on commercial aspects.

Scope

1. Corporate Lawyer, 2. Tax Consultant, 3. Legal Advisor (Finance), 4. Compliance Officer, 5. Company Secretary, 6. Contract Specialist, 7. Legal Auditor

Salary

₹3,50,000 - ₹7,00,000 /per year


B.Sc LLB

B.Sc LLB is an integrated undergraduate course that combines the study of Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) and Bachelor of Laws (LLB). It offers students a unique blend of scientific knowledge and legal expertise, enabling them to specialize in areas such as environmental law, patent law, or intellectual property law.

Scope

1. Patent Attorney, 2. Environmental Lawyer, 3. Intellectual Property Consultant, 4. Legal Analyst (Science), 5. Technology Lawyer, 6. Forensic Scientist (Legal), 7. Legal Researcher

Salary

₹3,50,000 - ₹7,00,000 /Per Year
